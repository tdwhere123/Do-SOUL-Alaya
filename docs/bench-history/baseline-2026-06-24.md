## What the 85.2 → 90 gap is (read before any recall PR)

- **Pure ranking, not retrieval.** Candidate-absent is ~1 question (pool@100 = 94.9%).
  The gold is in the pool; it is buried below rank 5 by `lexical_topic_neighbor` siblings.
- **any-gold@5 → 90 is reachable**: ~43 questions have the primary gold stuck at rank
  6–25; recovering ~23 of them reaches 90% (theoretical ceiling ~96%).
- **full-gold@5 = 20.7% is the real battleground**: the *secondary* gold of multi-gold
  questions is buried deeper (rank 11–100). Same disease (over-counted lexical-family RRF
  votes), deeper face.
- **Biggest single-category drag = preference any-gold@5 ≈ 63%** (from the 2026-06-15
  per-category diagnostics; this recall-eval kpi does not split by category). That is a
  query↔gold word-gap, **not** burial — its lever is a retrieval-tuned embedding
  (gte-small), not fusion de-correlation.
